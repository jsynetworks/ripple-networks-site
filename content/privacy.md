# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 16 March 2026**

## 1. Introduction
Ripple Networks ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. As a Jersey-based entity, we comply with the **Data Protection (Jersey) Law 2018**. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or engage our services.

## 2. Information We Collect
We may collect and process the following data:
* **Contact Information:** Name, email address, and phone number provided via email or contact inquiries.
* **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, and usage data collected via cookies or server logs.
* **Service Data:** Information provided during the course of IT consultancy or project delivery.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:
* Provide and manage our IT consultancy services.
* Communicate with you regarding inquiries, quotes, or ongoing projects.
* Comply with legal and regulatory obligations in the Bailiwick of Jersey.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing
In accordance with Jersey law, we process your data under the following legal bases:
* **Contractual Necessity:** To fulfill our service agreement with you.
* **Legitimate Interests:** For the effective management and promotion of our business.
* **Consent:** Where you have explicitly opted in (e.g., for marketing or newsletter subscriptions).

## 5. Data Retention & Security
We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy and to meet our professional record-keeping obligations. We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

## 6. Your Rights
Under the Data Protection (Jersey) Law 2018, you have the right to:
* Access the personal data we hold about you.
* Request the correction of inaccurate data or the deletion of your data.
* Object to or restrict the processing of your data.
* Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
